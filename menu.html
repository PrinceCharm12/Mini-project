FLAT WHITE COFFEE

AFFOGATO COFFEE
450 Calories

Affogato or "affogato al caffe", it is an Italian coffee-based dessert form of a scoop of ice cream topped or "drowned" with a shot of hot espresso.

LATTE MACHIATTO MILK COFFEE
170 Calories

Our latte macchiato consists of one 25 milliliter (0.84 ounce) shot of espresso, 150 milliliters (5-6.76 ounces) of steamed milk, and 1-2 dollops of milk foam. The steamed milk is poured into the glass first. It's followed by adding the milk foam to the top.

EINSPÄNNER
Viennese Coffee With Whipped Cream
160 Calories

Our Einspänner is served in a tall glass, with icing sugar and cocoa powder on top, it is not stirred, and the coffee should be slowly sipped through the whipped cream.

IRISH COFFEE
154 Calories

This Irish coffee is a caffeinated alcoholic drink consisting of Irish whiskey, hot coffee and sugar, which has been stirred and topped with cream. The coffee is drunk through the cream.

RED EYE COFFEE
45 Calories

The red eye coffee is simply brewed coffee topped with a shot of espresso. Most specialty coffee shops pull double shots, so this is usually a double shot of espresso.
